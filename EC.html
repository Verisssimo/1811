User Profile: Evelyn Carter

Name: Evelyn Margareth Carter

Date of Birth: January 15, 1780

Occupation: Advanced Researcher in Subatomic Matter.r

Location:Portland, Oregon.

Government Information: Evelyn Carter was a brilliant yet cautious woman. She was extremely dedicated to science, but over time, she developed a growing concern about the government's motivations and the potential risks that the experiments posed to humanity.

Her colleagues described her as discreet, yet with an insatiable curiosity, always exploring the depths of the unknown. Her last notes found in her laboratory suggest that she was on the verge of making a crucial discovery about the antineutrino and even about Hartmann's whereabouts.

Her relationship with Dr. Hartmann was one of deep respect and even admiration, but in the months leading up to his disappearance, she began to question whether they were truly in control of what they were creating.

Death:Her death was reported after the massive explosion at the Oregon laboratory, which involved research across various fields of science. Although her body was never found, experts and records confirm that Evelyn was present on the day of the incident with her team, following a long day of work.
